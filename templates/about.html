About Us

We are a digital museum initiative dedicated to bringing art history to life through conversational technology.

By integrating AI and curated content, we allow visitors to interact with artworks in a personal and immersive way—simulating real dialogue with the minds behind the masterpieces.

This project is part of a usability engineering & design exploration and aims to make art education more accessible, memorable, and inspiring.
