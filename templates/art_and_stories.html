Art & Stories

Step into a world where stories are told through brushstrokes and colors. Here you'll explore the inspirations, struggles, and legacies of iconic artists like Frida Kahlo and Jean-Michel Basquiat.

Each work of art in our museum comes with a story. We invite you to chat with the artworks themselves, discover their secrets, and experience history through their eyes.
